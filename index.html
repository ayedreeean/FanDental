Meet Dr. Fan

Dr. Benjamin Fan has lived in the Plano area for most of his life, and he is honored to have the opportunity to give back to the community. Dr. Fan attended University of Texas at Dallas and graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineer. He then completed his Doctor of Dental Surgery at the University of Texas at Houston Dental Branch. Dr. Fan believes his dental career is a lifelong journey where he continues to serve his patients wholeheartedly. His focus is for his patients to have a positive and honest experience. He strongly believes this journey coincides with his journey with God as a blessing. During his free time, Dr. Fan enjoys trying new food and dining out, snowboarding, running, badminton, basketball, and watching movies. He looks forward to meeting you in person for your dental health needs.
